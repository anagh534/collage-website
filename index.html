Welcome to Sa-adiya arts and science college!

Sa-adiya Arts and Science College, aiming at imparting education with a moral base, is a prestigious one among the 30 institutions functioning under Jamia Sa-adiya Arabiya. The college, established in 2003, is a self-financing college affiliated to Kannur University. The institution laid its foundation with the sole aim of giving quality education to meet the needs of society. It is open to the students of all sections irrespective of their religion, case, and gender providing amenities for education in a healthy atmosphere.
Â  Technology makes changes in lifestyle and the world rapidly into unimaginable ways. Reading these in the light of religious views makes Sa-adiya College of Science and Technology unique. Graduation level new generation courses in Biotechnology and Information technology are the highlights of this institution. An easily accessible and well-developed campus with modern facilities on the National highway is one of the prestigious components of Jamia Sa-adiya.
Sa-adiya provide variety of courses which are not frequently available in the Kasaragod district. It also provides an opportunity for aspirants in the IT field through providing BCA course, which helps students to take their degree in BCA without migrating to any other state or other district. Courses provided here are really affordable to everyone. There are different other courses provided here, under commerce department, courses provided are BCOM with COMPUTER APPLICATION (Bachelor of Commerce) and BBA(Bachelor of Business Administration). Other courses provided are BA ENGLISH, BA ECONOMICS, COMPUTER SCIENCE, BCA (Bachelor of computer application), B.Sc. BIOTECHNOLOGY, B.Sc. HOME SCIENCE. The college also provides Post Graduation (PG) course under commerce department that is MCOM (Master of Commerce). In each department Sa-adiya provides efficient and well qualified teachers with ensures high level of education and human values for each student studying in Sa-adiya arts and science college.
